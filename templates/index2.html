ABOUT US

Our website tool is a document-driven answering tool designed specifically for organizations seeking to analyze their data securely without risking the leakage of confidential information. This tool leverages several advanced language models and techniques including LLM (Large Language Models), Mistral 7b, Mini LLM, Llama CPP, and word embeddings. LLM models, such as Mistral 7b and Mini LLM, are state-of-the-art language models trained on vast amounts of text data. These models excel at understanding natural language and can provide insightful answers to a wide range of questions posed in natural language. By utilizing LLM models, our tool can analyze documents and extract valuable insights efficiently and accurately. Additionally, Llama CPP, which stands for "Large Language Model Anonymization for Confidentiality Preservation," plays a crucial role in ensuring the security of sensitive information. This tool allows for the anonymization of data before it is processed by the language models. By anonymizing the data, our tool can perform analysis without compromising the confidentiality of the organization's sensitive information. This feature is essential for organizations that handle confidential data and prioritize data security.
